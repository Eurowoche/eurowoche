The Eurowoche is made possible by the people who are part of it! Whether as a dancer, volunteer, or supporter of the association – you are warmly welcome.

## Dance with us {#tanz}

Would you like to dance with us and become part of our dance group? Whether with lots of experience or none at all – if you're interested, please contact our dance leadership.

[Contact dance leadership](/en/contact/)

## Volunteer {#helfe}

The European Youth Week is carried by dedicated volunteers. If you'd like to support us with organization, kitchen, technology or other tasks, please contact the board. Together we implement ideas and make the Eurowoche possible.

[Contact us](/en/contact/)

## Donate {#spende}

Your donation helps us keep the Eurowoche alive – a place where young people from all over Europe come together and develop mutual understanding. Every contribution, large or small, helps continue this tradition of friendship and peace.

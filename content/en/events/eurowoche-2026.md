The European Youth Week is a unique youth and folklore festival. Every summer, nearly 200 young people from at least four different European countries meet at Burg Ludwigstein to live together for a week, working, singing and dancing together. The young people showcase wonderful folklore from their home countries during their performances. Through workshops and various activities, they grow closer. Over the course of the week, a great community emerges – a European family.

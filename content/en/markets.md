With great dedication, the members of the association organize two special arts and crafts markets each year. The proceeds secure the financing of the European Youth Week and simultaneously create places of encounter for guests, exhibitors and volunteers.

## Easter Market – Crafts, spring customs and encounters

The Easter Market is an arts and crafts market organized by volunteer members of the association. The goal is to secure financing for the European Youth Week in summer through the proceeds.

Exhibitors from various regions of Germany present Easter customs and handmade products. Particularly popular is the artful egg craftsmanship, where miniature artworks are created from eggs ranging from ostrich to budgerigar size.

## Christmas Market – Light, music and winter atmosphere

On the second Advent weekend, Burg Ludwigstein transforms into an atmospherically decorated Christmas market. In the historic rooms, exhibitors present their handicraft works and provide insights into traditional manufacturing techniques.

A special highlight is the Christmas and nativity exhibition with exhibits from around the world. The association's band and other groups provide musical accompaniment.

Like the Easter Market, the Christmas Market is entirely organized by volunteers and contributes significantly to financing the European Youth Week.

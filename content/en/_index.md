Up to 200 young people from mostly European countries come together for a week to sing, dance, be creative and share their traditions through performances. Our festival provides space for genuine encounters, prejudices are broken down, and alongside beautiful memories, friendships form that often last well beyond the week. Discover this unique festival and learn how you can become part of it!
